# Code of Conduct

Mojify follows a simple contributor standard: be clear, be kind, and keep the work safe for people who are trying to help.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent when reviewing issues and pull requests.
- Give feedback on the code, design, or behavior, not the person.
- Make room for beginners and people debugging frustrating platform issues.
- Use reproducible details instead of insults or pile-ons.
- Respect maintainers' time and scope decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, slurs, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Posting exploit details publicly after being asked to use a private channel.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated low-effort demands.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or delete comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.
